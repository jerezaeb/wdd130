Bettamazing started by our family in 2022. Taking care of fishes started as a way of giving us relaxation from all the anxiety and stresses from the demands of life. Later on, these beautiful fishes made us appreciate God’s creation right before our eyes. We can’t get enough of buying fishes almost every week and then decided to breed betta fish and started our one stop fish pet shop.

We are committed to provide an all in one shop to provide the needs of your aquatic animals. We have competitive prices of fish tanks, pumps and filters, landscape accessories, fish food, water treatments and more. Our fish tank experts can provide you with valuable advice on how to best use the aquarium supplies we have available.

We offer fish lovers a wide range of unique and beautiful breed of fish that are perfect for both homes and offices. We also have a variety of fishes that we breed ourselves from common to rare type of Betta, Tetra and Guppies. We also supply these breed of fishes to retailers in the Philippines.

We ship aquarium fishes all over the Philippines. Contact us today!
